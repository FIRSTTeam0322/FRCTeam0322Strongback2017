LEFT_DRIVE_STICK
LeftDrive = Y-Axis +/- (Tank Drive)

Precision Control = Z-Axis (Throttle) +/-
*DriveTrain Brake/Coast Control = Button 1 (Trigger)

RIGHT_DRIVE_STICK
RightDrive = Y-Axis +/- (Tank Drive)


MANIPULATOR_STICK

A = Lift Robot
B = Stop Shooter
X = Shoot Balls
Y = Reverse Lift
LB = Stop Lift
RB = Turn off Brake 
*Select = Pickup Balls

* = Not Implemented
